## ğŸ“¢ Student Services

We operate various services to support your academic journey in the E3-1 Building.

* **Facility Management:** Management of student lounges, seminar rooms, and private lockers.
* **Event Support:** Organizing the 'Strawberry Party', 'Career Concert', and 'Hackathons'.
* **Academic Rights:** Addressing curriculum issues and acting as a liaison between students and the faculty.

> **Need Server Access?**
> If you are looking for help with `cp-service.kaist.ac.kr` or assignment submission protocols, please check the [Freshman Guide](https://www.google.com/search?q=%23) or refer to your course repository (e.g., `kaist-cp`).
